About Toybox

Toybox is a rock band based near London, UK.

The band was formed in 2007–2008 and has been performing and recording in St Albans (UK) and Liverpool (UK) since then.

The lineup is fluid, including Neil Oxtoby and friends.

For more info, and related bands, check out neiloxtoby.com/music.
